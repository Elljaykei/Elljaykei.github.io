Contender: The Chosen

Personal Rating: 8/10 | Rating: 7.6/10

Genre: Science Fiction, Young Adult

Another realm, another discovery and another mystery to unfold.
Cade is settling into a new boarding school, contemplating his future, when he finds himself transported to another realm; back in time with unknown technology.
This book is the first instalment of the Contender trilogy. If you were looking for more from the "Maze Runner" series, or an addition which you never knew you needed this is just the book for you.

Lord Of The Mysteries

Personal Rating 9/10 | Rating: 9.6/10

Genre: Mystery, Fantasy

Shrouded in the fog of history and darkness, who or what is the lurking evil that murmurs into our ears?
Waking up to be faced with a string of mysteries, Zhou Mingrui finds himself reincarnated as Klein Moretti in an alternate Victorian era world...
This book is, by no form of exaggeration, a book which has reached the peak of story development and legibility. The story flows smoothly and does not follow the clich√© plots which many books of similar genre follow. If you are a book enthusiast yourself and enjoy long reads, this is definitely a book for you.

The Black Tattoo

Personal Rating: 7/10 | Rating: 7/10

Genre: Young Adult, Paranormal

With great power comes great responsibility... or great consequence?
Charlie wakes up one day and finds a mysterious, moving black tattoo on his back...
The book touches an many elements like mystery, action and even lets you feel how the character feels with the detailed descriptions. This book only got a 7/10 for me as the story development may have been a bit rushed but overall is still a book that I would recommend you trying out if you like the supernatural genre.
