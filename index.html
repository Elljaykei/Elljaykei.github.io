TutorHub

Build Your Skills With Tutors And Learners Alike!

Due to Covid-19, many businesses were met with problems pertaining to the process of digitizing their business, moving from physical to a digital platform.

Here we are targetting the tuition sector and our answer to this is TutorHub. We made this platform by combining elements from famous platforms such as "Coursera" and "Fiverr".

Main Selling Point: Slight revamp to the business model with the mentality that everyone can be a learner. Removes worry stemming from the fluctuations of income due to peaks and troughs present in the business model.

Our solution was originally developed using a Python framework, Flask, now being developed using a JavaScript framework, ExpressJS.

CallforHelp

Problem Statement: With the increasingly aging population and a growing segment of vulnerable populations in mind, how might we leverage analytics for better sense-making to be alerted at the onset of incidents which require emergency response and mobilise CFRs for effective early intervention especially to the vulnerable population?

Solution: With the use of IBM Watson, a computer vision service, we created an application to decrease response time for emergency situations, in our case fire and heart attacks. Upon detecting either, there will be an alert sent to CFRs and professionals.
